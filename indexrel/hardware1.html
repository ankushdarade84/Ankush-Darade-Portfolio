"Advanced Communication System for Blind and Deaf People"

"The innovative system effectively bridges the communication gap between visually impaired and deaf-dumb individuals by leveraging Braille language and image processing technology. Equipped with a camera for image capture and a speaker for audio output, it operates in real-time to recognize names and convey messages audibly. Utilizing Raspberry Pi, a Braille Board, and a mobile phone, the system transcends barriers, facilitating seamless interaction between individuals with different physical challenges. Its multifunctional controller effortlessly interprets Braille text, converts it to speech, and performs image processing tasks to identify faces, ensuring enhanced accessibility and inclusivity."
