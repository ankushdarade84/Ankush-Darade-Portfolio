Smart Green House System

This is the implementation of a low-cost greenhouse management system for general users. We can control the parameters whether they should be increased or decreased. The data can also be saved to the cloud so that the data is always available for future analysis. This helps the farmers to control it better. The system enables the farmers to grow crops efficiently. The humidity threshold value also changes as per the crop requirement. Use of this system can provide better farming for the user. This will also help the crops to grow in every season and therefore can increase the production of farmers.
